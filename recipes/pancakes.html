Old-Fashioned Pancakes

Description

Craving a sweet morning breakfast? Well here are the ingredients to making good old fashioned pancakes

If this is your first time making pancakes no worries you might already have everything at home, if not here are a list of ingredients you will need

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg

      • Steps

      1. Sift the ingredients together
      2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combibe.
      3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan
      4. Cook for two to three minutes, then flip.
      5. Continue cooking until brown on both sides
