Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Ready to make a delicious homemade macaroni and cheese meal?

This irresistible comfort food will suprise you at how great it will taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons gound mustard
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups milk
  • 6 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (204 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Cook elbow macaroni in a large boiling pot stirring occasionally, until tender for about 8 minutes. Rinse under cold water. Drain well.
  3. Melt butter in on medium heat pot and sprinkle with flour, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt.
  4. Cook and stir until well blended for 2 minutes, then stir in milk and constantly stir unitl sauce thickens.
  5. Stir in cheese until melted and smooth. Turn off the heat then gently stir macaroni until coated.
  6. Transfer to the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle paprika over the top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and golden brown on top for 25 to 30 minutes, then let it cvool for 5 inutes before serving.
