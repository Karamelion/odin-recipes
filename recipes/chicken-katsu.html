Chicken Katsu

Description

Ready to make chicken katsu with this easy recipe?

You'll be blow away with how great it will taste with just these simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour.
  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko.
  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown.
